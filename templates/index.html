Welcome to Socratic AI

This AI uses a Socratic approach to guide your learning. It will ask probing questions to uncover misunderstandings and help you reason your way to deeper understanding. It won’t directly give answers; instead, it nudges you with questions and hints.

How to use

  • Select a subject from the dropdown.
  • Log in with your provided classroom username and password.
  • Ask your question or describe what you’re studying.
  • Answer the AI’s questions thoughtfully; it adapts based on your responses.
